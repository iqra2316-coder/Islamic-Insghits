Ziyarat Ashura
Imam Muhammad al-Baqir (AS) ne Alqama ibn Muhammad al-Hadrami ko sikhaya. Imam Ja'far al-Sadiq (AS) ne farmaya: "Jo koi 40 din tak yeh ziyarat padhe, ALLAH use woh sawab dega jo shaheed-e-karbala ke pehlu mein hoga."
Tarjuma:
اَلسَّلَامُ عَلَيْكَ يَا أَبَا عَبْدِ اللَّهِ
Salam — 100 martaba padhna
اللَّهُمَّ الْعَنْ أَوَّلَ ظَالِمٍ
Lanat — 100 martaba padhna
